The SANSQMonitor Component

A circular detector measuring the radial average of intensity as a function of the momentum transform in the sample.

Identification

  • Author: Martin Cramer Pedersen (mcpe@nbi.dk)
  • Origin: KU-Science
  • Date: October 17, 2012
  • Version: $Revision$

Description

A simple component simulating the scattering from a box-shaped, thin solution
of monodisperse, spherical particles.
WARNING: This is a contributed Component.
